Understand What You're Getting

Quality work means being clear about what actually happens to your car and why each step matters.

Two-Stage Hand Wash

I start with a heavy foam bath using a wash mitt to lift and remove the bulk of the dirt and grime. Then I come back around with a lighter foam application and work through every detail with quality microfiber towels and detailing brushes. I'm talking wheels, grills, decals, trim, door jambs, every corner and crevice. This isn't a quick rinse and go.

Clay Bar Decontamination

Over time, your paint collects contaminants that regular washing can't remove. Tree sap, industrial fallout, rail dust, road tar, overspray from construction. A clay bar treatment physically removes these embedded particles without damaging the paint. You can actually feel the difference - it goes from textured to glass smooth.

Interior Deep Cleaning

I'm getting into the seams, under the seats, between console gaps, all the places where dirt builds up. For Shield & Shine, the shampoo & extractor lifts dirt while boosting the color of your fabric and carpet. This is what removes the smells and stains that vacuuming alone can't touch.

Paint Protection

The 6-month sealant creates a protective barrier between your paint and the environment. Water beads up and rolls off, taking dirt with it. UV rays can't fade your paint as quickly. Bird droppings and tree sap won't etch into the clear coat if you clean them off within a reasonable time.
